BIO

Bringing the vibe of an unmatchable Rio de Janeiro sunset, with a very characteristic sound design and visual identity, the electronic music project Be Lion, created by DJ/Producer Henrique Telles, brings in form of music and style a unique blend of Rio de Janeiro, Ibiza and Mallorca.

Having his origin on tropical house, his first original track reached 600.000 streams. After this thunderous debut in partnership with the singer Kamatos, he was soon invited to release an EP thru the electronic music label of the giant Som Livre, Austro.

The pace of the EP could not be different, it surpassed the mark of the previous release and hit more than 1 Million streams. In the meantime he released music by Sony Music and introduced a new style to the brazilian market. Currently Be Lion has been turning his eyes and attention to the musical and visual style that fascinated him after his trip to Ibiza and are trendy on the hottest tracks on the Spanish island.

His new productions are becoming more and more the exquisite blend of the spanish house music made on Ibiza with the tech house creativity and identity of Rio de Janeiro.
